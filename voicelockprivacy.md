        <h2>2. Information We Collect</h2>
        <p>We prioritize data minimization. The Application collects the following types of information:</p>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Audio Data:</strong> Your voice input is processed in real-time to authenticate your identity.

            <li><strong>Usage Data:</strong> For the App Lock feature, we access information about which applications are being opened on your device. This data is used solely to determine whether an app should be locked and is <strong>never uploaded or stored</strong> outside your device.</li>
            <li><strong>Biometric Data:</strong> If enabled, the app uses your device's built-in biometric sensors (e.g., fingerprint). We do not have access to your actual biometric data; we only receive a confirmation of successful authentication from the system.</li>
            <li><strong>Device Data:</strong> We may collect non-identifiable information about your device, such as
                model, operating system version, and screen size, to ensure app compatibility and stability.</li>
        </ul>

        <h2>3. How We Use Your Information</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Authentication:</strong> To operate the core functionality of unlocking your device or specific apps using your voice or biometrics.</li>
            <li><strong>App Protection:</strong> To provide secure locking mechanisms for your installed applications via the App Lock feature.</li>
            <li><strong>App Improvement:</strong> To analyze app performance and improve user experience based on
                aggregated, anonymous data.</li>
        </ul>

        <h2>4. Permissions</h2>
        <p>To function correctly, the Application requires the following permissions:</p>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Record Audio:</strong> Essential for capturing voice commands for unlocking.</li>
            <li><strong>System Alert Window (Overlay):</strong> Required to display the lock screen over other apps.</li>
            <li><strong>Usage Stats:</strong> Required for the App Lock feature to detect when a locked app is being launched.</li>
            <li><strong>Biometric/Fingerprint:</strong> To allow optional biometric unlocking.</li>
            <li><strong>Post Notifications:</strong> To show the status of the lock service in your notification tray.</li>
            <li><strong>Read Phone State:</strong> Necessary to detect incoming calls and pause the lock screen,
                ensuring you can answer calls without obstruction.</li>
        </ul>
