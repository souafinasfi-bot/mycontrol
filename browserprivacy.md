# Privacy Policy for Bubble: Fast Floating Browser

**Last Updated: December 30, 2025**

Bubble: Fast Floating Browser ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### 1.1 Personal Data
We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) directly. We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, or contact details to use the app.

### 1.2 Browsing Data
Your browsing history, bookmarks, and session data are stored **locally on your device**. We do not have access to your browsing activity, nor do we transmit this data to our servers.

### 1.3 Third-Party Services (Advertising)
We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data, such as:
- Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID)
- Usage data and interaction with ads
- General location information (IP address)

This data is used to provide personalized ads and measure ad performance. For more information, please visit [Google's Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

## 2. Permissions

To provide our unique floating features, the app requires the following permissions:

- **Draw Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW)**: Essential for displaying the floating browser bubbles while you use other applications.
- **Internet Access**: Required to load websites and fetch advertisements.
- **Foreground Service**: Used to maintain your browsing session and the floating interface even when the main app is minimized.
- **Notifications**: Used to provide a persistent control for the floating service.
- **Storage (Read/Write)**: Necessary if you choose to download files or upload content through the browser.

## 3. Data Security
We prioritize the security of your data. Since your browsing data is stored locally, its security depends on your device's security. We recommend keeping your device updated and secured with a screen lock.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app.
